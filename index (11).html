Our Story

A family tradition, baked into every layer.

Mishpuchah — the Yiddish word for family — is at the heart of everything we do. Every cake that leaves our kitchen carries generations of love, authentic recipes, and the warmth of a home kitchen. We don't cut corners, we don't use shortcuts, and we never compromise on quality.

Homemade & Authentic
Every recipe is made from scratch using traditional family methods, passed down through generations.
Quality Ingredients
We source only the finest ingredients — real cream, fresh fruit, pure vanilla — nothing artificial.
Beautifully Crafted
Every cake is an edible work of art, designed to look as incredible as it tastes.
